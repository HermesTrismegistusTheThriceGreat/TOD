## Frequently Asked Questions

How can I join Alpaca Elite?

Sign up for an account and deposit $30,000 today. Happy trading!


Do I need to apply to receive the perks?

No, with a $30,000 deposit, you are eligible to switch to the Elite Smart Router. With a $100,000 deposit, you will automatically receive a lower margin rate, free market data subscription, and white-glove account support.


What is Elite Smart Router?

It is an institutional-grade electronic trading strategy offering cost-effective solutions with higher API limits and premium order types. For example, Alpaca will try to access dark and non-displayed liquidity before crossing the spread on marketable orders.


Does qualifying for Alpaca Elite automatically switch me to Elite Smart Router?

No, Elite users can stay commission-free as long as the order flows
remain retail in nature. [See\
here](https://alpaca.markets/support/what-flags-an-account-as-non-retail-and-what-are-the-implications-of-being-flagged) for factors that could flag a user as non-retail.


How does pricing work for Elite Smart Router?


All-In Plan: $0.0040 per share, encompassing all potential fees and
rebates (not including regulatory fees)

Cost-Plus Plan: $0.0025 or lower per share with volume tiering as a
commission on top of your pass-through fees or rebates. Depending on your
order flows, you may receive rebates that can offset the Alpaca commission
to lower your overall trading cost.
